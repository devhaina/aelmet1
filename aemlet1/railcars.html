AUTORACKS

We offer both bi-level and tri-level auto rack fleet for the transportation of motor vehicles.

Bi level auto racks are used for transportation of passenger automobiles, while tri levels are used for

transportation of SUVs, pickup trucks, and vans.

We also offer 89-foot aluminum bi levels, two-unit articulated bi levels, and two-unit articulated tri levels

that are each a unitized railcar design.

Commodities

  • Motor vehicles

Equipment

  • Bi-level rack
  • Tri-level rack

BOX CARS

We offer box cars in various height and length combinations. Box car fleet includes cars in lengths

TANK CARS

Tank car fleet includes both pressurized and general purpose tank cars for the transportation of liquids, liquefied compressed gases, or liquefied solids. Our tank car capacities ranges from 10,300 to 34,500 gallons, and are coiled, insulated, lined, and thermally protected.
