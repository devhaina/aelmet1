Spare And Components

Rail component products manufactured by AELMET serves the market through diesel engines, traction motors, locomotives, coaches, wagons, and forgings and castings. AELMET is capable of designing and manufacturing a comprehensive range of regular and customized components in given time frame. Our component is high quality, affordable, safe and meets international standards. Our component products are; locomotive components, diesel engines, traction motors, air compressors, turbochargers, water pumps, oil pumps, power/electric products, air springs, tight-lock couplers, draft gears, brake equipment and other custom made products.

With latest software, we provide service engineering such as reliability and safety improvement measures, re- engineering of various assemblies, so that the operation cost of the railway system can be brought down. We have got special competency to cater obsolescence management or impost substitution, if OEM is not supporting. For us each order is unique and we assure that customer’s needs and requirements are achieved.

AMET manufactures, rebuilds, services and supply traction motors and generators for locomotives, urban rail vehicles, oil drilling machines, mining machinery, and metallurgical machinery. We can re-engineer each motor and repair traction motors. We apply Innovative electromagnetic design, precise construction, insulation, and ventilation engineering to develop traction motors. AELMET manufactured traction motors are low in maintenance cost, highly reliable and extensive service life.

AMET is good at metallurgical sciences, when it comes to custom made ferrous and non-ferrous open dies, closed dies, rolled rings, and extruded forgings. We supply castings from a wide selection of materials, including carbon, low-alloy, high-alloy, stainless steel, gray, ductile alloy, non-ferrous alloy, iron, aluminum, zinc, and copper. We manufacture and supply affordable and high quality forged parts for railcars, including high quality crankshafts, connecting rods, pistons, water jackets, gears, couplings, cardan shafts, flanges and hubs conforming to international standards and customers’ specifications.

We use advance metal science and technology for specialized applications in forge bars, rings, hubs, blanks, step-down shafts, spindles, hollows, torch cut contours, and custom-shaped parts. Our high strength and long lasting parts have proven results in mechanical properties and structural integrity.
