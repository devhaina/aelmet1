Infrastructure Maintenance

1. Operation and maintenance of Rolling Stocks: Locomotives, Wagons and Coaches

1.1 Giving a target of 80% availability

1.2 Using right quality of spares

1.3 Predictive and Preventive Maintenance after onetime major overhauling

1.4 Midlife rehabilitation of Rolling stocks followed by Predictive and Preventive Maintenance

2. Maintenance of tracks

3. Maintenance of track Machines. Accident Relief trains and Cranes, etc.

4. Maintenance of Signaling System

Modernization of Rolling Stocks

1. Up-gradation of Locomotives: Replacement of DC traction motors with AC traction motors with AC Drive in existing fleet of Diesel locomotives. This will be done without replacing brake system.

2. Complete rehabilitation of Diesel Locomotives: provision of AC traction motors with AC Drive, Vehicle Control Unit, and Computer Controlled brake System, etc.

3. Design of new type of wagons and upgradation of existing wagons to suit commodities.

Enhancing Productivity by Empowering Manpower

Training and Modernizing of Human Resources Management System:

1. Offering Training Program for Locomotive Drivers, technicians, engineers, traffic managers, etc.

2. Training program to improve Reliability of Rolling Stocks (Locomotives, Coaches and Wagons)

3. Training program for Employees for improving of their Productivity.

Train Operation and Maintenance

1. Traffic Planning and Optimization of Capacity of Infrastructure

2. Complete Train Operation and Maintenance: providing manpower and materials, etc.

3. Supply of Quality Spares for Brake System

4. Overhauling and rehabilitation of DC Traction motors

5. Spares for Diesel engines.
