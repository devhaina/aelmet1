About Us

Applied Electrical and Mechanical Technology, AELMET Corporation is the leading rail road products and services provider. AELMET offers leading rail road technologies, engineering machineries, consulting services, and project management. AELMET has been serving transportation and rail road clients with R&D, design, manufacturing, repair, sale, lease and technical services. We meet our client’s requirements and expectations with our custom made world-class products like High-power locomotives, Bogie trucks, Passenger coaches, Wheels and axles, Brake disks, Test Equipment, Freight wagons, and track machineries. Whether it is complex geographical & environments challenges or diverse socio economic market, we have products and solutions for all.

Technological innovation and continuous

Technological innovation and continuous research and development have been our strength to envisage a safer, high quality, reliable, and affordable mode of rail connectivity across the world. We aim to attain sustainable development of railway transportation with our energy-efficient, eco-friendly, cost effective, intelligent, high speed and comfortable mobility.
