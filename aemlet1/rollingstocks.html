Rolling Stocks

AELMET offers designing, manufacturing, testing, commissioning and maintenance of locomotives and rolling stock, including electric locomotives and diesel-electric locomotives. We hold strong capabilities in manufacturing, customized maintenance and servicing of passenger coaches, freight wagons in ranges of covered wagons, open-top wagons, hopper wagons , flat wagons, double-deck container flat wagons, tanker wagons for all types of liquid and chemicals, tipper wagons, and depressed center wagons to meet market requirements. Built on proven design and engineering standards, all rolling stocks can easily be tailored to individual customer needs.
