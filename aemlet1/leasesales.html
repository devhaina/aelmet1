Leasing And Sales

Technological innovation and continuous research and development have been our strength to envisage a safer, high quality, reliable, and affordable mode of rail connectivity across the world. We aim to attain sustainable development of railway transportation with our energy-efficient, eco-friendly, cost effective, intelligent, high speed and comfortable mobility.

AELMET Leasing, Sales and Care Service are proven solution to our client's transportation needs. We supply customized locomotives, locomotive components, wagons, locomotive engines, traction motors, main generators, compressors, wheel sets, load regulators and other miscellaneous components.

We understand and acknowledge each client's requirements. We discuss and define alternatives in order to provide consulting for right locomotive, wagons, locomotive components, etc. to support the business of the clients.

We meet operational demands for railroads, industrial customers and short line carriers. Talk to our team and make AELMET your reliable partner for purchase or lease of any type of new or used locomotive, various types of wagons, locomotive components, locomotive engines, traction motors, main generators, compressors, wheel sets, and annual maintenance contract.

As part of Maintenance Service Agreements, our technical service and maintenance team is available on- site to repair, refurbish and upgrade your desired locomotive, wagons, motors, and engines to meet your operation needs.

If required, our service crew, travel to your site to repair/service/replace the required parts in your own shops under our supervision, ensuring the highest level of quality. Our contract maintenance & business assurance plans ensures that your rolling stocks fleet is expertly maintained to OEM standards.
